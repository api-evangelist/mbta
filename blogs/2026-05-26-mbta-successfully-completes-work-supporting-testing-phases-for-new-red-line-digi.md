The MBTA today announced the successful completion of critical infrastructure improvements accomplished along the Red Line Ashmont branch and Mattapan Line. The service suspension allowed the T to perform testing and commissioning work for the Red Line’s new digital signal system, track and tamping work, and major power infrastructure upgrades.
