The Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (MBTA) today announced the launch of the new F10 “Harbor Loop” ferry route, beginning Monday, June 29, 2026. The new route will provide year-round weekday service connecting North Station, Central Wharf / Aquarium, Seaport, and Logan Airport, expanding transit access and improving connections across Boston Harbor.
